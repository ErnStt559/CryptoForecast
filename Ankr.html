Price Predictions for End of 2025

Projections for Ankr (ANKR) by the end of 2025 vary based on market dynamics and infrastructure demand:

  • Bearish Scenario: ANKR could hover between $0.01 and $0.013 if competition intensifies or adoption slows. Some estimates suggest a low of ~$0.0134.
