Introduction

As of August 20, 2025, 01:19 AM EEST, EOS, now rebranded to Vaulta (AUSD), is trading at approximately $0.4859 USD, with a market capitalization of around $1.27 billion. Originally launched in 2018 by Block.one, EOS is a decentralized blockchain platform designed for high-performance decentralized applications (dApps) using a Delegated Proof-of-Stake (DPoS) consensus mechanism. In March 2025, EOS transitioned to Vaulta, pivoting toward a Web3 banking operating system, aiming to bridge DeFi and traditional finance (TradFi). This article provides a comprehensive analysis of Vaulta’s price outlook for the end of 2025, exploring its market trends, growth drivers, risks, and technical analysis based on current data and projections.
