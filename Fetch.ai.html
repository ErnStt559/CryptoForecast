Introduction

As of August 26, 2025, Fetch.ai (FET), now part of the Artificial Superintelligence Alliance (ASI), trades at approximately $0.64, positioning itself as a leading AI-powered blockchain platform. The ASI Alliance, formed by merging Fetch.ai, SingularityNET, Ocean Protocol, and CUDOS, aims to advance decentralized artificial intelligence across DeFi, supply chains, and IoT. The FET token, transitioning to the ASI token, powers autonomous AI agents and data-sharing services. This article provides a detailed price outlook for FET by the end of 2025, analyzing bullish and bearish scenarios, growth catalysts, and risks, supported by recent developments like a $50 million buyback program and a partnership with SuperWorld for decentralized AI in virtual worlds. coinmarketcap.com, coingecko.com

Current Situation

FET currently trades at ~$0.64, up 1.2% in the last 24 hours but 81.5% below its March 2024 all-time high of $3.45. Over the past 30 days, it has risen 13.3%, with volatility around 7–8%. The Fear & Greed Index shows greed sentiment, though specific scores vary. Recent updates include Phase 2 of Ocean Nodes with GPU support and hackathons like ICP x FET NextGen Agents. coinmarketcap.com, coingecko.com

Price Predictions for End of 2025

Price forecasts for FET by December 2025 vary due to market volatility and ASI integration. Below are key projections:

  • Bearish Scenario: Under selling pressure, Benzinga estimates FET could drop to $0.45–$0.48. Coinfomania suggests a low of $0.50, with a breakdown below $0.60 potentially triggering further declines to $0.38. benzinga.com, coinfomania.com
  • Moderate Scenario: CoinCodex predicts a range of $0.63–$0.67, while Changelly forecasts $0.84–$1.00, reflecting steady growth with ecosystem development. Binance posts indicate $1.00–$2.00. These suggest FET at $0.60–$2.00 with consistent progress. coincodex.com, changelly.com
  • Bullish Scenario: Optimistic projections include Coinpedia’s high of $10, Cryptopolitan’s $2.19–$2.69, and CoinLore’s $3.14. Community-driven estimates and market rebounds in Q1 2025 could push FET to $2.50–$5.00 if AI adoption and buybacks gain traction. coinpedia.org, cryptopolitan.com

Consensus range: $0.50–$5.00, with an average of $0.60–$2.50, depending on AI adoption and market trends.

  • Market Instability: A projected 30–50% crypto market correction in early 2025 could push FET to $0.50 or below, as seen in recent drops from $0.70 to $0.64. benzinga.com
