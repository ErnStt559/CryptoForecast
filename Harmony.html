Harmony (ONE) Price Prediction for End of 2025: Bearish vs. Bullish Scenarios

This information is provided for educational purposes only and is not financial advice. Cryptocurrency investments carry high risks.

Introduction

Harmony (ONE) is a layer-1 blockchain leveraging Effective Proof-of-Stake (EPoS) and sharding to deliver high-throughput, low-cost transactions for decentralized applications (DApps). As of late August 2025, ONE trades at approximately $0.0106, with a circulating supply of ~14.7 billion tokens and a market cap of ~$155 million. This is significantly below its all-time high of $0.38 in October 2021. The 2022 Horizon bridge exploit (~$100M) continues to impact investor confidence. This article provides an updated price outlook for Harmony by the end of 2025, analyzing bearish and bullish scenarios, technical indicators, and key ecosystem factors. coinpedia.org, coinmarketcap.com

Current Situation

Harmony’s current price is ~$0.0106, down 2.7% over the past 24 hours with a trading volume of ~$17 million. It has gained ~1.4% over the past week, indicating a modest recovery. Technical indicators are mixed: the Relative Strength Index (RSI) is ~44 (neutral), and ONE trades below its 200-day exponential moving average, suggesting no strong trend. The token remains range-bound with moderate volatility, far from its 2021 peak. coinmarketcap.com, coingecko.com

Price Forecast for End of 2025

Analyst predictions for Harmony’s price by the end of 2025 vary significantly, reflecting uncertainty in the crypto market. Below are the main scenarios:

  • Bearish Scenario: A sharp crypto market correction or stalled ecosystem recovery could push ONE to multi-year lows. TradingBeast forecasts ~$0.00735, WalletInvestor ~$0.01051, and Traders Union ~$0.00983 by December 2025. A 30–40% market drop could see ONE at $0.008–$0.010. swapspace.co, tradersunion.com
  • Moderate Scenario: Most models predict ONE trading near current levels. CoinCodex projects a range of $0.0106–$0.0116 (avg ~$0.0116), and Changelly estimates $0.0116–$0.0118 for December 2025. Traders Union’s midpoint (~$0.00983) aligns closely. This suggests ONE at $0.010–$0.012 with steady development. coincodex.com, changelly.com, tradersunion.com
  • Bullish Scenario: A crypto bull run, major upgrades, or DeFi/NFT growth could drive ONE higher. Coinpedia projects a high of ~$0.0413, and PricePrediction.net cites ~$0.0209. These assume strong market support, though $0.10+ is unlikely due to high token supply. Realistic bullish targets are $0.02–$0.04. coinpedia.org, swapspace.co

Realistic range: $0.008–$0.0413, with a likely average around $0.01–$0.02.

Factors Driving Growth

  • Protocol Upgrades: The June 2025 mainnet upgrade (v2025.1.1) improved consensus and P2P networking, achieving ~70% validator participation. Future upgrades (e.g., faster finality, Verkle trees, state expiry) could enhance network robustness. coinmarketcap.com
  • Yield & DeFi Strategies: New yield products, like BTC/USDC delta-neutral pools targeting 20–30% APR, increase ONE’s utility as collateral and attract capital. coinmarketcap.com
  • Ecosystem Innovation: Harmony’s 2025 roadmap includes pump.one (memecoin launchpad), exchange.one (perpetual/stablecoin exchange), and AI-driven Eliza OS on .country domains, expanding DeFi/NFT use cases. docs.harmony.one
  • Community & Validators: The “Pangaea” community spans 100+ countries, and a large, decentralized validator base supports governance and network security, fostering ecosystem growth. medium.com
  • EPoS and Sharding: Harmony’s EPoS caps validator stakes to prevent centralization, and its 4-shard network with ~2-second block times offers high throughput and low fees for DApps. docs.harmony.one

Risks and Downward Factors

  • Market Volatility: A 30–40% crypto market crash, as seen in 2022, could push ONE to $0.008 or lower, negating short-term gains.
  • Security Concerns: The 2022 Horizon bridge hack (~$100M) and related attacks (e.g., July 2025 CoinDCX hack) continue to erode investor confidence. coinmarketcap.com
  • Regulatory Uncertainty: Stricter global crypto regulations (e.g., KYC/AML, DeFi scrutiny) could limit user access and DeFi activity on Harmony.
  • Competition: Rivals like Ethereum, Solana, and Cardano compete for DApp and DeFi market share, potentially drawing developers away from Harmony.
  • High Supply: With ~14.7 billion ONE circulating, significant price increases require massive demand. A 100% rally would need a market cap jump from $155M to $310M. coinmarketcap.com
  • Developer Engagement: A ~19.5% month-on-month drop in GitHub commits in mid-2025 signals waning developer activity, which could stall ecosystem growth. coinmarketcap.com

Volatility and Technical Context

Harmony’s recent trading shows moderate volatility, with daily swings of a few percent. On August 26, ONE was down ~2.7% with a 24-hour volume of $17 million. The RSI (~44) is neutral, and ONE trades below its 200-day EMA, indicating no clear trend. It briefly broke above its 30-day SMA ($0.011) in mid-August but faces resistance at ~$0.0118 (23.6% Fibonacci level). Without a major catalyst (e.g., a crypto bull market or significant upgrade), ONE is likely to remain range-bound. coinmarketcap.com

Conclusion

By the end of 2025, Harmony’s price is likely to range between $0.008 and $0.0413, with a realistic average of $0.01–$0.02. Bullish scenarios (e.g., DeFi/NFT growth, protocol upgrades, crypto rally) could push ONE toward $0.02–$0.04, while bearish conditions (e.g., market crash, regulatory hurdles) may drive it to $0.008 or below. Reaching $0.10+ is unlikely due to the high token supply. Investors should monitor Harmony’s technical progress, security improvements, and market conditions. Thorough research and cautious risk management are essential before investing. For more details, visit harmony.one. coincodex.com, coinpedia.org
