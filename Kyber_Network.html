Kyber Network (KNC) Price Forecast for the End of 2025: Detailed Insights and Analysis

Introduction

Kyber Network (KNC) is valued at approximately $0.33. It is a decentralized liquidity protocol that facilitates seamless token swaps and DeFi integrations across multiple blockchains. With a market capitalization of around $56 million and a daily trading volume of about $6 million, KNC supports efficient cross-chain liquidity solutions. This analysis explores KNC’s price potential by the end of 2025, covering bullish and bearish scenarios, growth drivers, and risks based on market trends and ecosystem advancements.

Current Situation

Kyber Network (KNC) is trading at around $0.33, with a market capitalization of approximately $56 million and a circulating supply of ~170 million tokens. The 24-hour trading volume is ~$6 million, indicating moderate market activity. KNC is actively traded on major exchanges like Binance, Coinbase, and Kraken, primarily in USDT and USD pairs. Technical indicators suggest a neutral sentiment: the Relative Strength Index (RSI, 14) is near 50, with the price in a horizontal trend channel, signaling consolidation. Over the past year, KNC’s price has ranged from $0.20 to $0.40, with support at $0.30–$0.29 and resistance at $0.35–$0.38 and $0.40.

Price Predictions for End of 2025

Projections for Kyber Network (KNC) by the end of 2025 vary based on market dynamics and demand for DeFi liquidity:

  • Bearish Scenario: KNC could trade between $0.30 and $0.35 if DeFi adoption slows. Some estimates suggest a low of ~$0.3567.
  • Base Scenario: The expected range is $0.35–$0.42, with analysts forecasting an average of ~$0.38, driven by steady protocol enhancements.
  • Bullish Scenario: In a robust bull market with significant protocol upgrades, KNC could surpass $0.80, with optimistic projections reaching up to $0.85.

Consensus estimate: $0.30–$0.42, with an expected average of ~$0.38.

  • Protocol Innovations: Planned 2024-2025 updates enhance liquidity and DeFi tools.
  • DeFi Integrations: Growing liquidity pools and partnerships increase usage.
  • Cross-Chain Swaps: Enhanced interoperability across ecosystems drives adoption.
  • Market Momentum: Potential positive trends in late 2025 support growth.
  • Technical Advancements: Indicators suggest potential for steady gains in bullish conditions.

Risks and Downward Factors

  • Whale Influence: High concentration of 84% of supply among whales increases volatility.
  • Collateral Concerns: Reduced collateral ratios on exchanges like Binance signal caution.
  • Transaction Delays: Lack of instant transaction confirmations may impact usability.
  • Bearish Signals: Technical indicators reflect cautious market sentiment.
  • Competitive Pressure: Rivalry from other DeFi liquidity protocols.

Volatility Analysis

Kyber Network’s technical profile is neutral. The RSI (14) ≈ 50 indicates consolidation within a horizontal channel. MACD signals are mixed, with bearish sentiment from multiple indicators. Key support levels are $0.30–$0.29, with resistance at $0.35–$0.38 and $0.40. Trading volumes are moderate (~$6 million/day), with moderate volatility (7-day range $0.32–$0.34). KNC’s cross-chain capabilities support long-term potential, but whale concentration and competition may limit short-term gains.

Conclusion

By the end of 2025, Kyber Network (KNC) is expected to trade between $0.30 and $0.42, with a base case of ~$0.38. A bearish market could see prices at $0.30–$0.35, while a bullish scenario with protocol upgrades could push KNC above $0.80. Growth is driven by DeFi integrations, cross-chain swaps, and technical advancements, but risks include whale-driven volatility, transaction delays, and competition.
