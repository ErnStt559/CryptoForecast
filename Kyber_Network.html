Kyber Network Crystal v2 (KNC) Price Forecast for the End of 2025: Detailed Analysis and Outlook

The information in this article is provided for educational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice. Investing in cryptocurrencies carries high risks.

Introduction

Kyber Network Crystal v2 (KNC) is the utility and governance token of the Kyber Network protocol, a multichain hub for liquidity aggregation in decentralized finance (DeFi). The protocol aggregates liquidity from multiple sources, providing optimal token swap rates. KNC is used for paying fees, staking, and voting in KyberDAO, allowing holders to participate in governance and earn a share of network revenue. As of late August 2025, KNC trades around $0.37–$0.39 with a market cap of ~$73M. This article analyzes KNC’s price forecast for the end of 2025, reviewing current trends, growth factors, and risks.

Current Situation

As of late August 2025, KNC trades in the range of $0.37–$0.39 with a market cap of ~$73M (~187.6M tokens in circulation). Over the past week, the price dropped ~1.3%, while 30-day volatility was ~7.4%. Technical indicators show a neutral-bearish trend: RSI (~42) is near oversold, the 50-day SMA (~$0.427) is above the current price, and the Fear & Greed Index (~48) reflects neutral sentiment. 24h trading volume is ~$8–9M, indicating moderate activity. KNC is consolidating, with support at $0.35 and resistance at $0.44.

Price Forecast for End of 2025

KNC price forecasts for December 2025 vary depending on market conditions:

  • Bearish Scenario: In case of a 30–40% market correction or weak DeFi adoption, KNC may drop to $0.22–$0.30. WalletInvestor projects ~$0.22, viewing KNC as a poor long-term investment.
  • Moderate Scenario: With a stable market and gradual DeFi growth, KNC may trade between $0.35–$0.40. CoinCodex suggests an average price of ~$0.379 (range $0.348–$0.402), while 3Commas estimates $0.358–$0.380.
  • Bullish Scenario: Increased DeFi activity and successful protocol upgrades may push KNC to $0.44–$0.50. Changelly forecasts $0.435–$0.496, implying 20–30% growth.
  • Very Bullish Scenario: Prices above $1 are unlikely without a major technological breakthrough or strong bull market, requiring a market cap increase of several times.

Realistic range: $0.35–$0.50, with a base case around $0.40.

Growth Factors

  • Multichain Integration: Kyber Network operates across 13+ blockchains (Ethereum, Polygon, BNB Chain, etc.), aggregating liquidity for DEXs. New integrations and bridges boost KNC demand.
  • Protocol Upgrades: Planned KyberSwap v3 upgrades (2025) include gas savings, MEV protection, and high-APR pools (300%+ for ETH/USDT). This could attract more users and TVL.
  • KNC Burn Mechanism: A portion of network fees is converted into KNC and burned, creating deflationary pressure. Reduced supply may support prices as demand grows.
  • KyberDAO and Staking: Staking in KyberDAO allows holders to earn network fee revenue, incentivizing long-term holding and boosting KNC demand.
  • DeFi Growth: The anticipated DeFi boom in 2025 (especially in a bull market) may increase KyberSwap volumes, enhancing KNC utility.

Risks and Downside Factors

  • Market Volatility: KNC is sensitive to crypto market crashes. A 30–40% correction could lower its price to $0.22–$0.30, as forecast by WalletInvestor.
  • Competition: Uniswap, SushiSwap, and PancakeSwap dominate DEXs, offering similar features. Loss of liquidity to competitors may cap KNC’s growth.
  • Technical Risks: Delays or bugs in KyberSwap v3 upgrades (e.g., Katalyst) may erode trust and cause user outflow.
  • Regulatory Risks: Stricter DeFi and stablecoin regulation (e.g., EU MiCA) may limit Kyber Network’s usability in key markets.
  • Token Concentration: ~84% of KNC is held by large holders, posing risks of sharp sell-offs during market panic.

Volatility Analysis

Over the last 30 days, KNC showed ~7.4% volatility, trading between $0.35–$0.44. Support is at $0.35, resistance at $0.44–$0.50. RSI (~42) and MACD indicate a neutral-bearish trend, while the 50-day SMA ($0.427) above current price signals weakness. A breakout above $0.44 may open the path to $0.50, while a drop below $0.35 may test $0.30. Low trading volume and token concentration increase the risk of sharp moves under shifting market conditions.

Conclusion

By the end of 2025, KNC will likely trade in the $0.35–$0.50 range, with a base case of ~$0.40 under moderate DeFi growth and successful protocol upgrades. An optimistic scenario allows for $0.44–$0.50, but prices above $1 are unlikely without major catalysts. Risks such as competition, regulatory pressure, and token concentration may limit growth or push prices down to $0.22–$0.30. Investors should carefully analyze the market, follow KyberSwap’s development, and maintain diversification for risk management.
