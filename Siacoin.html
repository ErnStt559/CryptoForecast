Siacoin (SC) Price Prediction for End of 2025: Trends and Insights

This information is provided for educational purposes only and is not financial advice. Cryptocurrency investments carry high risks.

Introduction

Siacoin (SC) is the native token of the Sia network, a blockchain-based decentralized cloud storage platform launched in 2015. Sia allows users to rent unused hard drive space, paid for with SC, offering a secure, private, and cost-effective alternative to centralized providers like Google Drive or Amazon S3. As of late August 2025, Siacoin trades at approximately $0.0031, with a market cap of around $194 million, down 96–97% from its all-time high of $0.0929. With over 367 active storage hosts and a major V2 hard fork scheduled for June 2025, Siacoin is poised for potential growth. This article explores SC’s price forecast for the end of 2025, considering technical indicators, adoption trends, and risks. sia.tech, coingecko.com

Current Situation

As of late August 2025, Siacoin’s price is around $0.0031, with a market cap of $194 million and a daily trading volume of $7–20 million. The sentiment is neutral, with the Crypto Fear & Greed Index at 47–49. Siacoin’s 30-day volatility is moderate (~3–6%), with 17/30 days showing gains. Technical indicators include:

  • RSI (~45): Neutral, indicating no overbought/oversold conditions.

  • 50-day SMA: $0.00329; 200-day SMA: $0.00366, suggesting a flat trend.

The Sia network has over 367 active hosts and is preparing for a V2 hard fork in June 2025, introducing Utreexo for faster syncing and RHP4 for improved storage contracts. coincodex.com, coinlore.com, ainvest.com

Price Forecast for End of 2025

Analysts’ forecasts for Siacoin by the end of 2025 vary:

  • Bearish Scenario: A 30–40% market correction could push SC to $0.0028–$0.0046 (CoinDataFlow: $0.002896–$0.005947). coindataflow.com
  • Moderate Scenario: Steady adoption and network upgrades may drive SC to $0.006–$0.011. Coinpedia projects a low of $0.00455, average of $0.00817, and high of $0.0118; WalletInvestor ($0.00865); DigitalCoinPrice ($0.0108). ainvest.com
  • Bullish Scenario: Successful V2 hard fork and a crypto rally could lift SC to $0.014–$0.025, with some speculative estimates reaching $0.10–$0.11 (Godex). ainvest.com, godex.io

Realistic range: $0.004–$0.012, with a likely average around $0.008–$0.010.

Factors Driving Growth

  • Decentralized Storage Demand: Sia’s privacy-focused model (files split into 30 encrypted shards) appeals to users seeking secure storage. Growing data privacy concerns could boost adoption. messari.io
  • Stake-for-Access Model: Hosts must lock SC as collateral, tying network usage to token demand. messari.io
  • V2 Hard Fork: The June 2025 upgrade (Utreexo, RHP4) enhances scalability and usability, potentially attracting more users and developers. sia.tech
  • Sia Foundation Grants: Funding for ecosystem projects (e.g., renterd, hostd, browser access) strengthens utility. archive.sia.tech
  • Robust Host Network: Over 367 active hosts indicate strong network health, supporting SC demand. ainvest.com

Risks and Downward Factors

  • Market Volatility: A crypto market correction could push SC to $0.0028–$0.0046. ainvest.com, coindataflow.com
  • Competition: Filecoin, Storj, Arweave, and centralized giants (Amazon, Google) challenge Sia’s market share. messari.io, godex.io
  • Regulatory Uncertainty: Potential regulations or exchange bans could limit SC trading. messari.io
  • Upgrade Risks: Issues with the V2 hard fork could fragment the network or erode confidence. sia.tech
  • Liquidity: SC’s low price makes it susceptible to large swings from big trades.

Volatility and Technical Context

Siacoin’s 30-day volatility is moderate (~3–6%), with support at $0.00310 and resistance at $0.00357. The RSI (~44–46) and flat moving averages (50-day SMA: $0.00329; 200-day SMA: $0.00366) indicate a neutral-to-bearish trend without a breakout above $0.00357. A broader crypto rally could shift momentum, but a market downturn could test lower supports. coincodex.com, coinlore.com

Conclusion

By the end of 2025, Siacoin is likely to trade between $0.004 and $0.012, with a realistic average of $0.008–$0.010. A successful V2 hard fork and broader crypto rally could push SC toward $0.014–$0.025, while a bear market may see lows of $0.0028–$0.0046. Extreme predictions ($1+) are highly speculative. Investors should monitor Sia’s adoption, the June 2025 hard fork, and market trends while managing risks due to volatility, competition, and regulatory uncertainties.
