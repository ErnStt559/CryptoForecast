Introduction

Terra Classic (LUNC) is valued at approximately $0.000058. As the original token of the Terra blockchain following its 2022 collapse, LUNC is focused on a community-led revival through token burns and DeFi initiatives. With a market capitalization of around $320 million and a daily trading volume of about $20 million, LUNC aims to rebuild its ecosystem.
