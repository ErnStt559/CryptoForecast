Introduction

Price Predictions for End of 2025

Projections for Verasity (VRA) by the end of 2025 vary based on market dynamics and platform growth:

  • Bearish Scenario: VRA could trade between $0.0008 and $0.0010 if video ad tech adoption slows. Some estimates suggest a low of ~$0.0008.

Consensus estimate: $0.0008–$0.0013, with an expected average of ~$0.0011.
