Verge (XVG) Price Forecast for the End of 2025: Analysis and Outlook

The information in this article is provided for educational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice. Investing in cryptocurrencies involves high risks.

Introduction

Verge (XVG) is a privacy-focused cryptocurrency launched in 2014 under the name DogeCoinDark and rebranded as Verge in 2016. It uses a multi-algorithm Proof-of-Work blockchain and routes transactions through the Tor and I2P networks to conceal users’ IP addresses. Verge positions itself as a fast, low-cost, decentralized payment network suitable for everyday transactions. As of late August 2025, XVG is trading around $0.0059, with a market cap of ~$98M and ~16.52B coins in circulation. This article analyzes the price forecast for XVG by the end of 2025, considering market trends, technical indicators, and fundamental factors.

Current Situation

As of late August 2025, XVG trades around $0.0059 (rank #371 on CoinMarketCap) with a 24-hour trading volume of $4.78M–$7.43M. Over the past year, the price has dropped by ~22%, although it gained 50% in early 2025 compared to early 2024. Volatility over the past 30 days has been ~6.4%, with 40% “green” days. Technical indicators suggest a bearish trend: RSI (~39) is in the oversold zone, the price is below the 50-day SMA ($0.0066), but slightly above the 200-day SMA ($0.0053). Support is at $0.0058, while resistance lies around $0.009–$0.011. Low trading volume and high volatility point to instability.

Price Forecast for the End of 2025

XVG price forecasts for December 2025 vary depending on market conditions:

  • Bearish Scenario: In case of a market correction or regulatory restrictions, XVG may fall to $0.0016–$0.0060. CCN predicts a minimum of ~$0.0016, while Changelly forecasts $0.0058–$0.0060.
  • Moderate Scenario: Most forecasts point to a range of $0.007–$0.013. CCN expects an average of ~$0.009 (max $0.0137), Changelly an average of $0.00995 (max $0.0103), while Coinpedia suggests $0.0259 (range $0.00987–$0.03508). Consensus range: ~$0.008–$0.013, with a baseline of ~$0.01.
  • Bullish Scenario: With a crypto rally and rising demand for privacy, XVG could reach $0.02–$0.035. Coinpedia forecasts a maximum of $0.035, while CoinLore suggests as high as $0.2365 (unlikely without extreme hype).

Realistic range: $0.008–$0.013, with a baseline of ~$0.01.

Growth Factors

  • Privacy demand: Verge leverages Tor and I2P to provide transaction anonymity, appealing to users seeking privacy. Rising interest in private digital currencies may boost demand for XVG.
  • Crypto bull market: The expected recovery after Bitcoin’s halving (April 2024) may lift altcoins, including XVG. Breaking key resistance levels ($0.009–$0.011) could drive prices to several cents.
  • Listings and integrations: New exchange listings (e.g., XVG/USDT on Digifinex in April 2025) and integrations (e.g., SwapSpace) improve liquidity. Inclusion in CoinMarketCap’s “Made in the USA” list raises awareness.
  • Transaction speed and cost: Verge offers high throughput (>100 TPS) and low fees (0.1 XVG), making it attractive for everyday payments.
  • Community and development: An active developer team and community support upgrades like stealth addresses and cross-chain tokens. Partnerships (e.g., with MindGeek) and marketing campaigns boost credibility.

Risks and Downside Factors

  • Regulatory restrictions: Privacy coins like Verge face regulatory pressure. AML/KYC restrictions or exchange delistings (as happened with Monero and Zcash in Japan and South Korea) could shrink XVG’s market.
  • Competition: Monero and Zcash have larger ecosystems and stronger reputations, potentially diverting capital from Verge. Criticism from Monero developers undermines trust in XVG’s technology.
  • Past vulnerabilities: The 2018 attack (rapid block creation and theft of millions of XVG) damaged trust. New vulnerabilities could trigger sell-offs.
  • Bearish signals: Price below the 50-day SMA ($0.0066), RSI ~39, and lower Bollinger Band ($0.0058) indicate weakness. Failure to break resistance ($0.009–$0.011) may lead to further decline.
  • High volatility: Volatility of ~6.4% over 30 days and a history of sharp drops (–97% from the $0.30 peak in 2017) create downside risk during market downturns.

Volatility Analysis

XVG’s price history shows high volatility (6.4% over 30 days), with 40% “green” days. The price is near the lower Bollinger Band ($0.0058), RSI (~39) indicates oversold conditions, and the 50-day SMA ($0.0066) above the current price signals weakness. Support is at $0.0058, resistance at $0.009–$0.011. A bull market or positive news could push the price to $0.01–$0.013, but current indicators suggest risk of further decline without catalysts.

Conclusion

By the end of 2025, XVG will likely trade in the $0.008–$0.013 range, with a baseline of ~$0.01. A rise to $0.02–$0.035 is possible in a strong bull market with growing privacy demand, but forecasts above $0.20 (e.g., CoinLore) are unlikely without extreme conditions. Risks include regulatory restrictions, competition, and past vulnerabilities. Investors should monitor market trends, new listings, and protocol updates, while conducting thorough research before investing.
