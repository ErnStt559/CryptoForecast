Current Situation

Internet Computer (ICP) is trading at around $3.25, with a market capitalization of approximately $1.5 billion and a circulating supply of ~480 million tokens. The 24-hour trading volume is ~$300 million, reflecting active market engagement. ICP is traded on major exchanges like Binance, Coinbase, and Kraken, primarily in USDT and USD pairs. Technical indicators suggest a bearish sentiment: the Relative Strength Index (RSI, 14) is near 50, within a falling trend channel indicating a medium-term bearish outlook. Over the past year, ICP’s price has ranged from $2.50 to $4.50, with support at $3.00–$2.80 and resistance at $3.50–$4.00 and $4.50.

Price Predictions for End of 2025

Projections for Internet Computer (ICP) by the end of 2025 vary based on market dynamics and adoption trends:

  • Bearish Scenario: ICP could trade between $3 and $4 if adoption growth stalls. Some estimates suggest a low of ~$4.37.
  • Base Scenario: The expected range is $4–$15, with analysts forecasting an average of ~$8, driven by ecosystem expansion.
  • Bullish Scenario: In a robust bull market with strong Web3 adoption, ICP could surpass $30, with optimistic projections reaching up to $40–$50.

Consensus estimate: $4–$15, with an expected average of ~$8.

  • Market Cycle Momentum: Potential growth during the 2025 post-halving cycle.
  • Web3 and AI Adoption: Increasing use in decentralized applications and AI integrations.
  • High Upside Potential: Benefits from volatility and market opportunities.

Volatility Analysis

Internet Computer’s technical profile is bearish. The RSI (14) ≈ 50 indicates consolidation within a falling trend channel. MACD shows a bearish trend, though a slight recovery is noted.
