Internet Computer (ICP) Price Forecast for the End of 2025: Comprehensive Analysis

The material in this article is intended for educational purposes only and should not be considered financial advice. Cryptocurrency investments carry high risk.

Introduction

Internet Computer (ICP), developed by the DFINITY Foundation, is a Layer-1 blockchain aimed at decentralizing the internet and supporting Web3 applications. ICP is a decentralized computing platform that allows the deployment of canister smart contracts across a global network. Its key feature — chain-key cryptography — enables virtually unlimited scalability, high throughput (over 770k TPS), and sub-second transaction finality, providing a “gas-free” user experience. This allows for the development of full-fledged decentralized applications (SocialFi, GameFi, DeFi) with both frontend and backend on-chain. As of late August 2025, ICP is trading around $5.3–$5.8 with a market cap of ~$2.8–$3.1 billion. The Fear and Greed Index (~71, "greed" zone) indicates optimism despite volatility. internetcomputer.org, binance.com, gate.com

Price Forecast for the End of 2025

Forecasts for ICP at the end of 2025 vary depending on market conditions and project progress. Main scenarios:

  • Low scenario: In case of a global market correction or development setbacks, the price may drop to $4.8–$5.3. For example, Gate.com forecasts a possible decline to ~$4.84, while WalletInvestor points to a minimum of $5.33. gate.com, walletinvestor.com
  • Base scenario: Under stable conditions, ICP may trade in the $6–$12 range. CoinCodex projects $4.95–$6.48 (ROI ~29%), while 3commas estimates an average of $5.52. coincodex.com, 3commas.io
  • Optimistic scenario: In a bull market with successful innovations, the price could reach $18–$38. Coinpaper forecasts $7–$37.8 (average ~$18), while InvestingHaven suggests $20.2 with institutional support. Coinedition.com projects up to $36. coinpaper.com, investinghaven.com, coinedition.com

Consensus range: $4.8–$18, with a likely average of $6–$12. coincodex.com, coinpaper.com

Growth Factors

  • Ecosystem and DeFi growth: TVL in ICP protocols increased 458% since early 2024, reaching $123.41 million, indicating growing activity and capital inflow. binance.com
  • Partnerships: Collaboration with Elliptic (2025) ensures compliance for institutions, while integration with Binance and Chain Fusion expands cross-chain capabilities with Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana. elliptic.co, dfinity.org
  • Technological innovations: Chain-key cryptography provides scalability and instant finality. Chain Fusion allows interaction with other blockchains without intermediaries. On-chain AI support (models up to ~500 GB) enables unique use cases. binance.com, dfinity.org
  • Web3 infrastructure: Full-stack blockchain applications, HTTP support, and reverse gas payments make ICP attractive for SocialFi, GameFi, and DeFi developers. binance.com

Risks and Limiting Factors

  • Market volatility: A 30–50% correction could lower the price to $4–$5, especially if the overall market declines. gate.com
  • Regulation: Stricter rules in the US and EU may limit ICP’s availability, leading to capital outflows. coinpaper.com
  • Competition: Ethereum, Solana, Aptos, and other Layer-1 blockchains may attract developers and investors if they introduce new innovations. coinpaper.com
  • Historical volatility: After peaking at $750 in 2021, the price dropped to $2.87 in 2023, undermining investor confidence. coinpaper.com

Volatility Analysis

In 2025, ICP’s 30-day volatility was ~4.8%, with the price rising from $5.30 to $5.79 in July–August (~9.3%). RSI (~60–65) and the price moving above the 50-day SMA (~$5.15) indicate a neutral-to-bullish trend. Support is at $5.05, resistance at $6.08–$7.33. The large circulating supply (~535 million tokens) smooths out sharp fluctuations, but rising TVL and demand create potential for an upward trend. Global market waves may amplify moves. gate.com, coinlore.com

Conclusion

By the end of 2025, ICP’s price will likely range between $6–$12 in the base scenario, with upside potential to $18–$38 in a bull market, driven by technological innovations and ecosystem growth. Conservative forecasts point to $4.8–$6 in case of correction. Investors should consider risks of volatility, regulation, and competition, carefully monitoring news and market conditions.
