Tezos (XTZ) Price Forecast for the End of 2025: Comprehensive Analysis

The content in this article is intended for educational purposes only and should not be considered financial advice. Investing in cryptocurrencies involves high risk.

Introduction

Tezos (XTZ) is a decentralized blockchain platform with a self-amending protocol that uses on-chain governance for upgrades without hard forks. It supports smart contracts, DeFi, NFT, and blockchain gaming, competing with Ethereum and Cardano. The Liquid Proof-of-Stake algorithm allows validators (“bakers”) with 6,000 XTZ and token delegation. Tezos is known for its ICO ($230 million) and NFT ecosystem (Objkt, fxhash). As of late August 2025, XTZ trades around $0.73 with a market cap of ~$768M. coinmarketcap.com

Current Market Situation

As of August 30, 2025, XTZ trades around $0.73 (~58 RUB), with a market cap of ~$768M and a circulating supply of ~1.056B tokens. Over the past week, the price dropped ~6–8%, with moderate volatility. Technical indicators: the 50-day SMA crossed below the 200-day SMA, but the price remains below both (200-day EMA ~$0.70), indicating a neutral-to-bearish trend. RSI (14) ~49–50, near neutral. Support levels: $0.70 and $0.65; resistance: $0.78–0.80 and $0.83–0.85. XTZ is available on major exchanges (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken). ru.beincrypto.com, coinedition.com

Price Forecast for the End of 2025

Forecasts for XTZ in December 2025 vary:

  • Bearish scenario: Price may drop to $0.22–$0.50. CCN predicts $0.22–$0.32 under negative market conditions. ccn.com
  • Base scenario: Price in the $0.75–$0.80 range. Changelly expects an average ~$0.779, CoinCodex ~$0.787. changelly.com, coincodex.com
  • Bullish scenario: Price may reach ~$0.84–$0.85 with strong ecosystem development. Changelly projects a maximum of ~$0.841. changelly.com

Consensus range: $0.75–$0.80, with upside potential to $0.85 or downside to $0.22 in the worst case. changelly.com, coincodex.com

Growth Drivers

  • DeFi and Layer-2: Etherlink attracts DeFi protocols (Curve Finance, Reaper Actual, Kolibri, SpicySwap), boosting the ecosystem. coinmarketcap.com
  • NFT and culture: Tezos hosts collections from McLaren, Red Bull, GAP on Objkt and fxhash. Museums (Serpentine, d’Orsay) experiment with NFTs. coinmarketcap.com
  • On-chain governance: The 5-phase upgrade cycle (e.g., “Ithaca”) ensures flexibility and community trust. coinedition.com
  • Institutional adoption: Hex Trust tokenizes assets (xU3O8), Ubisoft and DOGAMÍ use Tezos for gaming. coinmarketcap.com
  • Staking: Liquid Proof-of-Stake with ~4–5% annual reward incentivizes long-term holding. coinmarketcap.com

Risks and Limiting Factors

  • Technical failures: Bugs in smart contract code or during upgrades could undermine trust. coinedition.com
  • Competition: Ethereum, Cardano, Solana, and Avalanche may attract users and capital, especially in DeFi and NFT. coinmarketcap.com
  • Declining dApp interest: Falling activity in DeFi and NFT may hurt demand for XTZ. ccn.com
  • Regulatory risks: Strict crypto regulations may restrict access to dApps and reduce XTZ’s appeal. coinmarketcap.com

Technical Analysis

XTZ is in a descending channel. RSI (~49–50) and MACD show bearish momentum. Price is below the 50-day and 200-day SMAs, with support at $0.70 (200-day EMA) and $0.65. Resistance: $0.78–0.80 and $0.83–0.85. A breakdown below $0.65 could lead to $0.50–0.60, while a rise above $0.80 would signal reversal. Sustained buying volume is needed for a bullish trend. coinedition.com, ru.beincrypto.com

Conclusion

By the end of 2025, XTZ is likely to trade in the $0.75–$0.80 range, with a bullish scenario up to $0.85 and a bearish scenario down to $0.22–$0.50. Growth drivers: DeFi (Etherlink), NFT (Objkt, fxhash), institutional adoption (Hex Trust, Ubisoft), and staking. Risks: competition, declining dApp activity, and regulatory constraints. Investors should monitor protocol upgrades, DeFi/NFT growth, and key levels around $0.70–$0.80. For more information, visit coinmarketcap.com and changelly.com.
